Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temp

The above graph displays the max temps for all given latitudes. From this data we can conclude that temperatures over 90 degrees, and under 0 degrees F, are very rare for any latitude. The most prevalent temperatures for our dataset being between 60 and 80 degrees. The cities recording the very lowest temperatures occur over 70 degrees latitude, and under -40 degrees latitude. As our data moves towards the middle of the earth the extreme-low temps begin to become more infrequent.
