Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

Based on the above graph we can gather that extremely high percentages of humidity are far more common than low percentages. Cities with humidity percentages below 60 are fairly low in number, and those with extremely low humidity only occur between latitudes of -40 and 80 degrees. Also of note is that there is no city with a humity of 0%, but there are quite a few with that of 100%.
